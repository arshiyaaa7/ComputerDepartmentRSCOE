Department Details

Vision

To strive for excellence in Computer Engineering education by developing competent engineers with research, innovation and leadership skills.

Mission

  • To empower students with fundamentals and emerging technologies in Computer Engineering.
  • To promote industry-institute interaction and entrepreneurship skills.
  • To inculcate professional ethics, leadership, and lifelong learning attitude.

Program Educational Objectives (PEOs)

  • Graduates will be able to apply knowledge to solve real-world problems.
  • Graduates will demonstrate leadership and teamwork skills.
  • Graduates will pursue higher education, research, or entrepreneurship.
